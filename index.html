How it works

Built around your practice routine.

Zyntune fits naturally into how you already practice — no complicated setup, no learning curve.

1

Set your daily goal

Choose how many minutes you want to practice each day — 15, 30, 60, or custom. Zyntune keeps you on track.

2

Start the timer

Hit start when you sit down to practice. Stopwatch or countdown — whatever works for your session.

3

Log your session

Save your session with notes and tag the piece you worked on. Your history builds automatically over time.

4

Watch your streak grow

Come back tomorrow and keep the streak alive. Consistency is the secret to mastery — Zyntune makes it visible.

2

Every Studio gets a unique code. Share it with students however you like — text, email, or print it on a syllabus.

3

Students join

Students enter the code in their app and instantly connect to your Studio.

4

Create assignments with suggested practice times and track progress from your dashboard.
